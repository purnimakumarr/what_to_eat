What To Eat (Food Picker)

What to eat is a webpage which helps you decide what you should eat for a meal. It lets you enter your food choices and then picks a meal for you and suggests recipes and restraunts near you.

How it works

Three easy steps

1

Enter meal choice and press Add meal button.

2

Repeat the process until all your choices are entered.

3

Press Select meal and the food picker fill choose a meal.
