What To Eat?

Cannot decide what to eat or want to make your meal a little interesting?
Enter options for what you want to eat and get a randomly generated meal for you.

How to use:-

  1. Enter an option of foof you would like to eat.
  2. Click the Add Meal button to add the option.
  3. Repeat the above two steps until you enter all your options.
  4. Click the Choose Meal to get a random pick from your options.

Fun tip: Challenge your friends to eat a meal chosen from a pool of options entered by you.
You can be as creative with the options as you want :)
