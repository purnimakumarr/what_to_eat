# What to eat?
A webpage to choose a random food item for your meal.

## How it works?
| Steps       | Description                                            |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------|
| Step 1      | Enter meal choice and press Add meal.                  |
| Step 2      | Repeat the process until all your choices are entered. |
| Step 3      | Press Select meal                                      |

You can also look up for recipe ideas and/or restraunts for the meal by clicking the links provided in the page. Hope you enjoy your meal ðŸ˜Š.
